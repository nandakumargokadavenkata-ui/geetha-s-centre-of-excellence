Our Story

A Passion for Teaching
That Changes Lives

Geetha's Tuition Center was established in 2016 with a vision to provide strong academic foundations through disciplined teaching and individual attention — from LKG all the way to Class 10.

2016
Humble Beginnings
Geetha Mam founded the center in Allipuram, Visakhapatnam, with a small batch of students and a powerful vision: every child deserves patient, concept-based guidance.
2018
Growing Trust
Word spread across Allipuram and neighbouring areas. Parents recognised the difference that small batches and individual attention made in their children's performance.
2021
Steady Through Challenges
Continued serving students through difficult times, maintaining the same discipline, concept clarity, and personal attention that defines Geetha's Tuition Center.
2024
Trusted Local Institution
Today, Geetha's Tuition Center is a trusted name in Visakhapatnam — guiding students from LKG to Class 10 with care, discipline, and clarity.
